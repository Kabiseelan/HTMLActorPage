vijay

Vijay was born on 22 June 1974 (age 49, as of 2023) in Chennai, India. He is one of the highest paid actors of Kollywood (Tamil Cinema). Be it his acting, singing or dancing; the multitalented actor excels in all the fields. Not just in India, he has his fan following in China, Japan, UK, and France.
